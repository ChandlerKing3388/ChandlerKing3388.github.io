Orange Julius

  • Time:10 Minutes Max
  • Serves:4-6 People
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 1 Cup
Vanilla Extract 2 Teaspoons
Frozen Orange Juice Concentrate 16 ounces
Sugar 1/2 Cup
Ice 11/2 Cups

Step 1: Blend the milk and vanilla

Step 2: Add the frozen concentrate

Step 3: Add the sugar and ice cubes and blend

Step 4:Pour and enjoy

Spagettie Noodles Through Hotdogs

  • Time:30-40
  • Serves:6-8 People People
Ingredients Quantity
Spagetti 1 Box
Hotdogs 1-2 Packages
Butter Half a teaspoon

Step 1: Cut the hotdogs horizontally in little sections

Step 2:Poke the spagetti noodles through the hotdogs

Step 3: Pour spagetti and hotdogs into a pot of boiling water

Step 4:Put half teaspoon of butter into pot and cook for 18-20 minutes

Step 5:Poor spagetti noodles and the hotdogs into a strainer

Step 6:Enjoy

Crapes

  • Time: 30 Minutes
  • Serves:4-6 People
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 1 Cup
Eggs 2
Milk 1/2 cup
water 1/2 Cup
Salt 1/4 Teaspoon
Melted Butter 2 tablespoons

Step 1: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and the eggs. Steadily add the milk and water while mixing. Finish off by adding the salt and butter and beat until smooth

Step 2:heat/warm up a frying pan over the stove at medium tempature. Poor the batter into the frying pan about 1/4 a cup each time. Finish off by tilting the pan in a circular motion to help spread batter evenly

Step 3: Cook unil light brown on bottom and then use a spatula to flip and cook on the other side

Step 4:Put it on a plate and add what ever toppings you want. Enjoy
